# User Manual

## Overview

The Bombcrypto Marketplace is the official platform to buy and sell Hero and House NFTs using BCOIN or other supported tokens.

## Connecting Your Wallet

1.  Click the **Connect Wallet** button in the top right corner.
2.  Select your wallet provider (e.g., MetaMask, WalletConnect).
3.  Approve the connection request in your wallet.
4.  Ensure you are on the correct network (BSC or Polygon).

## Buying NFTs

1.  **Browse Listings**: Use the filters on the left sidebar to find the Hero or House you want. You can filter by Rarity, Level, Stats, and Price.
2.  **Select an Item**: Click on a card to view detailed stats.
3.  **Purchase**:
    -   Click the **Buy** button.
    -   Confirm the transaction in your wallet.
    -   Wait for the blockchain confirmation.
4.  **Success**: Once confirmed, the NFT will appear in your inventory in the game.

## Selling NFTs

1.  **Go to Inventory**: Navigate to your inventory page on the marketplace.
2.  **Select Asset**: Choose the Hero or House you wish to sell.
3.  **Set Price**: Enter the price you want to list it for.
4.  **Approve**: If this is your first time selling, you may need to approve the marketplace contract to spend your NFT.
5.  **List**: Confirm the listing transaction.
6.  **Manage**: You can cancel your listing at any time from the "My Listings" section.
